Color Contrast Checker

Check the contrast of foreground and background colors with WCAG 2.0 relative luminance and WCAG 3.0 APCA (advanced perceptual contrast) formulas.

Supports hex, rgb, hsl, hsv, hsluv, lab, lch, and jab with alpha

Contrast ratio:

Large text is 18pt or 14pt bold. Small text is less than 18pt or less than 14pt bold.

Try generating color by desired contrast ratio instead.
